Understanding RRIFs in Ontario

A Registered Retirement Income Fund (RRIF) is a tax-deferred retirement account that provides regular income payments. In Ontario, strategic RRIF management can significantly impact your tax burden and overall retirement income.

You must convert your RRSP to a RRIF by age 71, and minimum withdrawals are required starting the year after conversion. However, smart planning can help you optimize these withdrawals.

  • Tax-deferred growth until withdrawal
  • Flexible withdrawal amounts above minimums
  • Option to use spouse's age for calculations
  • Various investment options available
  • Income splitting opportunities in Ontario
